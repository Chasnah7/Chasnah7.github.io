Miscellaneous Sites

Other web sites with great information and resources.

  • TheOldNet - are you tired of this new internet yet? Get THEOLDNET!
  • FrogFind! - can't browse the internet on that Macintosh SE anymore? Try FrogFind!
  • 68k.news - the only news source you can trust (to keep your dialup costs low).
  • TextFiles - massive archive of BBS text files, some works originally dating back to the 1960's.
  • CD TextFiles - Another TextFiles site for archiving shareware CDs.
  • Nathan's Toasty Technology Page - Learn about the evils of Internet Explorer.
  • DOSBox DeathMatch Club - an organization for regular deathmatch events of classic PC games through DOSBox.
  • Blue OS Museum - a web site dedicated to reviewing pre-release builds of operating systems, as well as OEM recovery discs.
  • Floodgap - offical web site of Floodgap Systems. Hosted by Cameron Kaiser.
  • Super Dimension Fortress - public access UNIX system.
  • GopherSpace Tutorial - page hosted by SDF with information about the Gopher protocol, the forgotten internet.
